School Tours

When I was applying to college, I didn’t have the chance to visit any campuses in person, which made the process feel even more uncertain. Visiting a school is such an important part of truly getting to know it and helping you decide where you belong. After I started college, I was fortunate enough to visit some UC campuses and tour with current students. Hearing their stories and experiences gave me a deeper understanding and connection—something I wish I had before making my own decisions. That’s why I hope you can have that chance early on and feel more confident about your path!
